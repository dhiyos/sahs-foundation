# 01 — Brief

## What we are building

A static marketing website for **SAHS Foundation**, the humanitarian arm of the SAHS Group of Companies. The site introduces the foundation, explains its eight core missions, demonstrates impact, and provides clear pathways for donors, corporate partners, and volunteers.

## Who it is for

| Audience | What they need from the site |
|---|---|
| **Individual donors** | Quick understanding of what SAHS does, where their money goes, and how to donate |
| **Corporate CSR partners** | Credibility signals, scale, transparency, partnership pathway |
| **Volunteers** | What initiatives exist, how to get involved |
| **Beneficiaries / referrers** | Easy way to reach out for assistance |
| **Media / press** | About info, press kit, contact details |

## Why we are building it

The foundation currently has a brochure but no digital presence. A website is needed to:

1. Establish credibility and legitimacy for an organisation that channels SAHS Group profits into philanthropy
2. Open a corporate CSR partnership pipeline
3. Provide a 24/7 information point for donors and beneficiaries
4. Document and showcase impact

## Tone & personality

- **Dignified** — never pity-driven or transactional
- **Warm** — community-led, human-first
- **Credible** — backed by a multinational group, not donation-dependent
- **Transparent** — 100% transparency, zero profit motive

## Key differentiator vs. typical NGOs

Unlike donation-dependent NGOs, SAHS Foundation is **funded by SAHS Group profits**. This is a major trust signal and should be surfaced prominently. The site is not a fundraising machine — it is a credibility and partnership tool.

## Out of scope (for v1)

- Online donation gateway integration (link out to a payment page instead)
- Member login / donor portal
- Multi-language (English only for v1)
- Blog / CMS (static content, edited via code for v1)
- Live impact dashboard

## Success metrics

- Time on site > 90 seconds
- Bounce rate < 55%
- Contact / partnership inquiries via the form
- Lighthouse score: Performance ≥ 90, Accessibility ≥ 95, SEO ≥ 95
